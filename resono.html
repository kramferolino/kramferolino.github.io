Business Process Outsourcing

RESONO is a Business Process Outsourcing service (BPO), designed and managed specifically for servicing businesses from Australia and New Zealand. Broadly speaking, a BPO service is the contracting of your operations and responsibilities of a specific function within your business to RESONO, as a third party service provider. You give us the work, we do it for you.

RESONO aims to keep things as simple as possible, and catagorise our functions into either a Low, Medium or Complex back of house (BOH) banding - which includes your internal business functions such as:

  • Financial admin - accounting, payroll and bookeeping.
  • Application validation/processing
  • Software testing
  • IT and helpdesk support
  • General admin
  • Data management
  • HR
  • Claims processing

Or front of house (FOH) banding - which includes customer-related services such as:

  • Lead qualification
  • Customer relationship management
  • NPS (Net Promotor Score)
  • Customer surveys
  • Mystery shopping
  • Setting appointments
  • Consumer enquiries
  • Live chat / moderation

Features

When choosing to offshore your business there are a number of considerations that need to be made: location, local infrastructure, operating costs, culture, political stability and economic variables.

The Philippines is the market leading location in the BPO industry because of:

  • Excellent local IT infrastructure.
  • Government investment and policy favoring BPO operations.
  • Lower attrition rates in comparison to other offshore markets.
  • A service orientated culture with uniquely westernised value system in South-East Asia.
  • Market leading cost advantages.
  • Excellent English speaking capability, with high tertiary qualification graduates in the workforce.

Based on these features, RESONO's BPO solution in Manila will deliver a significant competitive advantage to your business in terms of both cost and quality of work delivered.

RESONO is dedicated to quality solutions and low risk transition strategies for clients and we maintain an Australasian management team on site in Manila to conduct regular, transparent communications with you, dedicated to ensuring our results exceed your expectations.
